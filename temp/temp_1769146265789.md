# Using Modern OCR In Extracting Report 

## Abstract 

Optical Character Recognition (OCR) plays a critical role in digitizing academic literature, nabling search,analysis,and knowledgeextraction from sanned documents.



## I ntroduction 

However, academic papers present unique challenges to OCR systems due to complex layouts, multi-column formats, mathematical symbols, and varied font styles.

## Conclusion 

This study evaluates the performance of a modern OCR pipeline on single-page academic documents.
